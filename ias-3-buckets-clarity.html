Why The Confusion Happened

The Overlap

Both Bucket 1 and Bucket 3 involve "business planning" and "AI tools" — the language sounds the same even though they're different things.

The Trigger

Demoing the Coach4U app inside an IAS session blurred the line between "what we're building for you" and "what I've built separately."

The Fix

Be explicit going forward: "This is IAS work (Bucket 1)" vs "This is something I offer as a separate product (Bucket 3)." Clear labels, clear boundaries.
